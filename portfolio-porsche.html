Tanner Harrington - Park Place Porsche Sales Experience Manager
This project is the personal, professional website for one of Park Place Porsche's Sales Experience Managers, Tanner Harrington. The website was designed and developed in one month, and includes an original logo, an interactive landing page, methods to view Tanner's current Park Place Inventory, a section about the Sales Experience Manager, and links to contact him; I also drew up several banner ad concepts for him to be able to use. Below, you can see the main screens.
Challenging Goals
Tanner contacted me with a website in mind that was simple, but also informative, interactive, and intuitive for users. He also wanted an original logo that resembled Porsche, while also having unique individuality to him. Additionally, I was to come up with several banner ad concepts for him to potentially use.
Problems Creatively Solved
Many personal car salesmen websites are boring, counter-intuitive, and have too many sections to navigate to and from - leaving the user stuck, and not sure where to go. In my simple yet elegant design, the user is able to immediately view inventory to see what they are looking for, read briefly about the salesmen, and contact him immediately at multiple domains.
